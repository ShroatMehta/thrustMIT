OUR PROJECTS

We build sounding rockets for SpacePort America Cup. Our team has evolved and improved after each project of ours. Let's see what have we built.

LAPWING

Lapwing series was our first attempt at rocketry, it was used to test our initial ideas and prepare for our bigger launches.

VYOM

Vyom is the first sounding rocket build by thrustMIT.Weighing at 26 kgs, Vyom has a apogee of 1.2 Km. The airframe is made up of Glass fibre and has Aluminium 6061 T6 bulkheads.

ARYA

Arya is our second sounding rocket with a carbon fibre airframe, weighing at 24kgs with a height of 251 cm. With upgraded avionics, Arya has a payload capacity of 4kgs and an apogee of 3Km.

PHOENIX

Pheonix is our first sounding rocket with a completely modular design. Pheonix sports carbon fibre airframe and fibreglass avionics bay to facilitate telemetry. It also has Exterior coating on the fuselage to improve outer surface finish and better rocket aerodynamics.
