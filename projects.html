Our Projects

P H O E N I X

Specifications
Weight : 36 Kg
Diameter : 150 mm
Apogee : 3 Km
Payload : VibLab
Recovery System : In-house Reefing
Fuselage Material : Carbon Fiber

To be launched in Spaceport America Cup 2021, Phoenix has a carbon-fiber airframe and glass fiber avionics bay to facilitate telemetry and communications.


It uses aluminum bulkheads and an internal modular support structure for easy assembly and interchangeable bays. A smooth exterior coating on the fuselage improves the outer surface finish for better rocket aerodynamics. Add in a nose tip of aluminum, and it can reach transonic speeds.


The payload is a suite of electronics and instruments that use Piezoelectric materials to gauge vibration at different locations in the rocket during flight. This data is invaluable for optimizing the rocket components in the future.

A R Y A

Specifications
Weight : 24 Kg
Diameter : 140 mm
Apogee : 3 Km
Payload : Non-Newtonian Damper
Recovery System : In-house Reefing
Fuselage Material : Carbon Fiber

Arya, launched in Spaceport America Cup 2019, is made entirely of carbon-fiber. In the design, we incorporated aluminum bulkheads, carbon fiber, and centering rings to improve structural performance.


Lapwing 2 is a subscale rocket that tested all the design concepts and final systems used in Arya's design. An in house reefing mechanism facilitates easier recovery of the rocket.


The payload is a closed vessel carrying Non-Newtonian liquid. It aimed to observe the damping behavior of the fluid during flight, which was when a large amount of acceleration changed the fluid properties.

V Y O M

Specifications
Weight : 26 Kg
Diameter : 160 mm
Apogee : 1.2 Km
Payload : Dead Weight
Recovery System : COTS Reefing
Fuselage Material : Glass Fiber

Vyom is our first rocket, launched in Spaceport America Cup 2018. It has a glass fiber airframe with aluminum bulkheads and centering rings. A nose tip of ABS finishes the aerodynamic profile.


Lapwing 1 is a subscale rocket that tested all the design concepts and final systems used in Vyom's design.


The payload is dead, much like a paperweight. It has no function.
